Louisville Personal Injury Attorneys

Lawyers You Can Count on When You Get Hurt

Whether it happens at work, on the road, or at the grocery store, becoming injured as a result of someone else’s negligence never feels good.

If you've been injured, the Louisville personal injury attorneys of Walker Wallace PLC can help you seek compensation for medical expenses, pain and suffering, lost wages, property damage, and more. We are a local law firm that has represented clients in personal injury and workers' compensation cases, and we’re dedicated to helping our clients find relief and compensation after being injured.

If you or someone you care about has been injured due to to someone else's negligence, you may be entitled to compensation. We handle injury cases resulting from car crashes, bicycle accidents, trucking accidents, nursing home abuse and neglect, workplace incidents, and more.

Additionally, our attorneys have experience helping clients obtain workers' compensation benefits for injuries on the job, qualify for Medicaid, and create comprehensive estate plans.
